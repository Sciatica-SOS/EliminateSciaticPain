1. The Key Details

Created by Glen Johnson, a former chronic sufferer of sciatica, “Sciatica SOS” is an informative guide that shares an ancient Nepalese remedy that was designed to help you get rid of sciatica-related pain permanently in just a week or less.

A Quick Background

According to Glen Johnson, his doctor diagnosed him of sciatica when he was 41 years old. The pain he felt was so excruciating that his legs would literally tighten up. The different pain meds, anti-inflammatories, and muscle relaxants worked for him, but only for a short while. Worse, they have numerous side effects, and his chiropractor sessions didn’t do him any good either.

Glen says all that changed when his wife’s old Nepalese friend named Xie, who used to be a traditional healer, visited him at home. She gave him a 2000-year-old remedy that got rid of the stiffness of his legs and finally helped him become free of sciatica.

In the Sciatica SOS guide Glen Johnson shares everything that helped him get rid of his sciatica permanently, and more specifically, here is a sneak peek of what’s inside the manual:

– All the information you need to know in order to take advantage of the ancient all-natural home remedy to get rid of sciatica-related pain. To make the remedy even more effective, Glen also provides instructions on how to add it to your daily diet together with specific herbs, so you can flood your body with a sense of calm.

– All the steps you need to know in order to cure and restore balance to your joints, muscles, tendons, and nerves every time you experience sudden pain due to your sciatica.

– Glen also discusses other important things regarding sciatica, like why it’s so prevalent and what the excruciating pains are telling your body.

Aside from the main guide, the Sciatica SOS package also contains these six additional bonus:

Bonus #1: How to Lose 10 Pounds Naturally – As what the name says, this bonus guide teaches fast and effective ways to lose weight and maintain a healthier lifestyle despite your busy schedule.

Bonus #2: Stress Soothers – In this guide, you’ll learn different methods to manage stress, as well as how to identify and prevent your triggers.

Bonus #3: 10 Ways to Fight Off Cancer – This free guide contains ten tips on how you can lower your risk for cancer.

Bonus #4: Sleeping Solace – Inside, you’ll find a list of sleep-promoting foods, what you should do before going to bed, and the best environment to get relaxing sleep every night.

Bonus #5: Lessons from Miracle Doctors – This eBook on natural health contains powerful information on how to become and stay healthy.

Bonus #6: Unlimited Lifetime Updates – Glen Johnson explains that he is constantly researching the latest on sciatica treatment. If ever he releases a new version of the Sciatica SOS guide, he’ll send it to you immediately via email for free…

 

2. An In-Depth Look At The Pros And Cons

 

The Main Pros

 

Natural And Effective Treatment

We’re not saying you shouldn’t try conventional treatments. They do work for some people. However, studies have shown that meds prescribed to ease the discomfort of sciatica produce zero to small improvements. For instance, steroids are no better than a placebo pill. [1]

If you don’t want to undergo any surgery or take a cocktail of meds, it wouldn’t be a bad idea to try natural alternatives. There are studies that have shown that natural methods, like acupuncture, produce significant positive results.

The great thing about the Sciatica SOS program is that it doesn’t only suggest natural solutions, but it also targets the root cause of sciatica.

Easy To Implement

The main guide itself is easy to comprehend. There are no complicated jargons or confusing instructions, and it’s easy to incorporate the remedy into your everyday life, too.

Improved Quality Of Life

How many years have you been suffering from sciatica?

Based on our research online, users of Sciatica SOS had to endure this condition for a few months to years. However, many of them reported that after using Glen Johnson’s natural system they experienced improvements in just 24 hours. They say they were able to get a proper night’s sleep, able to move their body without the excruciating pain, and resumed their normal activities worry-free.

Useful Bonus Items

This is another thing we like about Glen Johnson’s Sciatica SOS system. You get five bonus guides and lifetime updates at no extra cost, and we personally believe that these bonuses can be very useful for any sciatica sufferer.

60 Days Full Refund Guarantee

Like other natural treatments, the Sciatica SOS can’t promise a 100% success rate. However, the good news is that if within sixty days you’re still experiencing serious bouts of pain, Glen Johnson says he is more than willing to give you back every penny you spent.

Clickbank processes the refunds of the Sciatica SOS system, so you can rest assured that your refund request is going to be processed…

 

The Main Cons

 

Information About The Author

We couldn’t find useful information about Glen Johnson online. Perhaps it’s a pen name. We’re not really sure.

However, while it’s a little frustrating, we believe it doesn’t automatically mean he’s a fraud or that his guide is bogus. After all, many credible authors do hide under different aliases these days…

Sciatica SOS Is Available In Digital Format Only

The Sciatica SOS manual and the five bonus reports are available in PDF format which you can instantly download after your payment. If you don’t like reading eBooks, this would prove to be a disappointment on your part.

Of course, there’s always that option of printing everything at home by yourself…

Overall, we believe the Sciatica SOS guide is worth checking out if you’re searching for a natural way to get rid of your sciatica.

One thing we find appealing about this is that it offers a natural approach to treating sciatica, so you can get relief from the debilitating pain without taking numerous meds. Conventional painkillers give only temporary relief anyway.

In addition, if you’ve been to the official Sciatica SOS website, you’ll find at the bottom of the page the ‘Doctor Trusted Website’ seal. That’s a very important thing, and it means that an independent doctor – in this case, it’s Dr. Michael Woo-Ming, MD – carefully scrutinized the medical information, claims, service terms, policies, and the product itself and found the website meeting the strict requirements set by the Doctor Trusted Code of Conduct…

Of course, there are also some downsides to Sciatica SOS, and you can’t expect to get the same results (or any results) as others because each person is different. In addition, the Sciatica SOS program is not a complete replacement to your doctor’s advice, and if you’re suffering from a bad case of sciatica, we think that it can be a good idea to talk with your doctor before you start following Glen Johnson’s system.

On the other hand, there is no doubt that Sciatica SOS can be a very helpful resource for many sciatica sufferers and upon further reflection, we concluded that Glen is not really making any false claims. Sciatica is really curable. It’s just a matter of finding the right solution that works for your unique needs.

To sum it up: Sciatica SOS is a useful guide which gets the average rating of 8.5/10 from us. While we can’t assure you that it would work for you 100%, we can promise that you can try it out risk-free because of Glen Johnson’s 60-day refund guarantee, which Clickbank stands behind….

That’s everything we could say about the Sciatica SOS system by Glen Johnson. We hope we have provided you with the information you needed, and we sincerely wish you a pain-free and happy life!
